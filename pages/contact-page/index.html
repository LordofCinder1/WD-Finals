Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and have experience managing remote projects effectively. I use modern collaboration tools to ensure smooth communication throughout the development process.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, and various databases. I choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and goals.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! I offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly. We can discuss the best support plan for your needs.

How do you handle project payments?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment and 50% upon completion for smaller projects. Larger projects are broken into milestones with payments distributed accordingly. All terms are clearly outlined in our contract.

Can you help with existing website improvements?

Definitely! Whether you need bug fixes, new features, performance optimization, or a complete redesign, I can help improve your existing website or application.
